The challenge

Serving clients on WhatsApp at scale is hard. Without the right tools, it breaks.

Your clients already message you on WhatsApp, and they expect to be served there. That part is settled. The hard part is doing it consistently across dozens or hundreds of simultaneous conversations — without things slipping, without burning out your team, and without losing the personal quality of the interaction.

That's exactly what WA Copilot is built for.

Handle more without losing quality

A structured pipeline and AI-assisted replies let your team handle significantly more conversations at the same standard — without dropping a single thread.

Full visibility across every conversation

See every open request, every waiting case, every resolved one. Know exactly what's pending, who's been waiting longest, and what needs attention now.

Escalate instantly when it matters

Complex cases shouldn't stall waiting for someone to forward a chat. Loop in a manager or specialist with one click — full context, no copy-paste, immediate resolution.

No client ever falls through the cracks.

When a client messages again, WA Copilot automatically moves them back to New — even if they were marked Resolved. If you need to follow up later, snooze puts them back on your radar at exactly the right moment.

  • Auto-reopen the moment a new message arrives
  • Snooze: defer a conversation until the time is right
  • Full message history always one click away
  • Search any message with ⌘F inside the chat

Privacy

Your conversations never leave your Mac.

There is no WA Copilot backend. Your messages travel from WhatsApp directly to your screen — without passing through any external server.

Read the full source code →

💾
Local SQLite database
All your messages and contacts are stored on your Mac. You control the backup.
📂
WhatsApp session on disk
Your WhatsApp session lives in the app's local folder — on your Mac only, protected by macOS file permissions.
🚫
No third-party servers
Your Mac talks directly to WhatsApp. No data passes through anyone else's infrastructure.
👁
100% open source
Every line of code is on GitHub. Verify exactly what runs on your computer.
