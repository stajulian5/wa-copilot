The problem

WhatsApp wasn't built for client service at scale.

As a KAM, WhatsApp is your primary channel. But it's also where you get memes, family photos, and group chats you forgot you joined. The important conversations disappear into the noise.

There's no native way to know who is waiting for a response, who is mid-process, or who you haven't contacted in weeks.

Conversations buried and forgotten

A client asked something three days ago. You saw it, thought "I'll reply later" — and it sank to the bottom of your list. You never got back to them.

No pipeline visibility

How many clients are actively in process? How many haven't heard from you in over a week? WhatsApp doesn't give you that view.

Replying at volume is exhausting

When you have 50+ active conversations, every reply starts from zero. No quick context, no smart templates, no help.

Pipeline

See where every client stands at a glance.

Every WhatsApp conversation lives in a column. Drag contacts forward as relationships progress — no spreadsheets, no sticky notes.

  • 4 stages: New, Open Conversation, Waiting For, Resolved
  • When a client replies, the card jumps back to New automatically
  • Unread badge count always visible on the dock icon
  • Search any client by name with ⌘K

Continuity of service

No client ever falls through the cracks.

When a client messages again, Copilot automatically moves them back to New — even if they were marked Resolved. If you need to follow up later, snooze puts them back on your radar at exactly the right moment.

  • Auto-reopen the moment a new message arrives
  • Snooze: defer a conversation until the time is right
  • Full message history always one click away
  • Search any message with ⌘F inside the chat

Setup

Up and running
in 3 minutes.

No Terminal. No technical configuration. Just download and scan.

Download & install

Open the .dmg, drag WhatsApp Copilot to Applications, and launch it. macOS verifies it automatically — no warnings.

2

Open WhatsApp on your phone → Linked Devices → Link a device → point the camera at the code. Just like WhatsApp Web.

Privacy

Your conversations never leave your Mac.

There is no Copilot backend. Your messages travel from WhatsApp directly to your screen — without passing through any external server.

Read the full source code →

💾
Local SQLite database
All your messages and contacts are stored on your Mac. You control the backup.
🔑
Session in Keychain
Your WhatsApp session is stored in macOS Keychain. Encrypted, local, inaccessible to anyone else.
🚫
No third-party servers
Your Mac talks directly to WhatsApp. No data passes through anyone else's infrastructure.
👁
100% open source
Every line of code is on GitHub. Verify exactly what runs on your computer.
